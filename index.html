Hi! I am Yu Zhang (张宇/張宇), a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU). I am fortunate to work with supervisor Dr. Zhicong LU (陆志聪/陸志聰).

Before my Ph.D. study, I received my bachelor's degree in computer science from Beihang University (BUAA) in 2017, and a master's degree in computing from The Australian National University (ANU) in 2019. From Oct. 2019 to Apr. 2021, I worked at Thoughtworks (Nasdaq:TWKS) as a software developer.
